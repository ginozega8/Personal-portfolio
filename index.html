How did I learned?

At first, I got interested in programming because since I was a little child I had a close relationship with computers, I loved surfing the web, reading things, watching videos, and of course playing video games. However, until last year, I never had the courage to get into programming and actually create something with a computer, because I was scared of mathematics and I wasn't good at them. But one day I said "let's do it", and I realized that every fear I had about programming dissipated and I realized that I liked it. At first, I started doing the App Academy software development course, which gave me the bases of programming and the Ruby language. Later I switched to web development because it was the branch of programming that caught my attention the most, so I started learning the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at Alura academy. After I finished that course, I found the FreeCodeCamp resources and started learning more complex JavaScript and CSS, libraries, and frameworks, and I'm currently learning Backend to become a full-stack. I study 7 days a week between 4-6 hours because I'm decided to get better at what I do.
