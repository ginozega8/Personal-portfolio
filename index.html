About me

Hi, my name is Gino, I'm 21 years old and I'm a self-taught Full-Stack developer looking for my first IT job. I'm experienced in the MERN stack, but I'm interested in working with any modern technology. Speaking about myself, I consider myself a perfectionist person and have quite a lot of attention to detail, so I always try to make my code as clean, reusable, and maintainable as possible. I also consider myself a fast learner, I adapt to new technologies efficiently to implement them in my routine correctly and quickly, a trait that makes me feel comfortable in this industry, which is constantly changing, mutating, and updating the landscape

How did I learn?

At first, I got interested in programming because since I was a little child I had a close relationship with computers, I loved surfing the web, reading things, watching videos, and of course playing video games. However, until 2021, I never had the courage to get into programming and actually create something with a computer, because I was scared of mathematics and I wasn't good at them. But one day I said "let's do it", and I realized that every fear I had about programming dissipated and I realized that I liked it. At first, I started doing the App Academy software development course, which gave me the bases of programming and the Ruby language. Later I switched to web development because it was the branch of programming that caught my attention the most, so I started learning the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript at Alura academy. After I finished that course, I found the FreeCodeCamp resources and started learning more complex JavaScript and CSS, libraries, and frameworks. I'm currently doing projects on my own to get better at what I do. I study 7 days a week between 4-6 hours because I'm decided to get better at what I do.
